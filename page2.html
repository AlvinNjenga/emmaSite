About Me

Emma Porter

I am 6 foot tall and I typically wear UK sizes 14-16. I have broad shoulders and wide hips, placing me into the hourglass body type. I aspire to model to help create more diversity in our media, a cliche statement, yes, but no less true than when it was first said. Young girls who grow that bit bigger and taller need to know they are normal, and if they grow taller than boys they need to know that it's absolutely bloody normal and pretty kick ass - S.O to 12 year old Emma! I don't know everything, I don't know much at all to be honest, but what I do know is there is barely anyone that looks like me in the media. That NEEDS to change, even if that responsibility is not for me to change, it does need to start cha. We expect our media to represent us but if the media are too slow in representing us then we've got to do it ourselves. Like my mum says, if you want it done properly do it yourself, so that is what I'm doing! I'd love to work with companies and people that just want and appreciate real representation. Thank you for taking time to look at my portfolio, I look forward to hearing from you and possibly working with you, and if I'm not what you're looking for at this time, I hope I can be in the future. Thank you, Emma x
